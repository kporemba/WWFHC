TOURNAMENT DETAILS

The summer turf tournament is a recreational tournament that runs in mid-July. It is a festival style tournament where no trophies or prizes are won. Individual players, or teams up to 17 players can be entered. Up to 10 teams can be entered with priority given to those who enter first. The tournament rules can be found here.
