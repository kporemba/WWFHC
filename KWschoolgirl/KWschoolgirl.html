TOURNAMENT DETAILS

The Kitchener-Waterloo School Girls Tournament is held anually starting Saturday September 14th 2019, and is a two day tournament. It is round robin based, with approximately 4 games played per each team (25 minutes each), not including playoffs - counting on the same number of teams registering again this year. It is currently limited to 1 entry per team, until June 1st 2019 where more entrees are permitted if spots are still available.

Registration is first come first serve with spots usually filling up by May, it is not recommended to wait if you want to enter a team. The tournament rules can be found here.
